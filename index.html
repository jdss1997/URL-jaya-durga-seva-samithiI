👋 Welcome to JDSS Youth

Since 1997 | Tradition Continues till 2025 & Beyond

📍 Kodimail, Jagityal District – 505501

JDSS Youth (Jaya Durga Seva Samithi) is a symbol of devotion, culture, and youth power. With the blessings of Maa Durga and Lord Ganesh, we bring people together in joy, devotion, and brotherhood.

🙏 Our Celebrations

  • 🕉 Durga Mata – Devotional puja, cultural programs, rituals, music, and traditional activities.
  • 🐘 Ganesh Chaturthi – From installation to Visarjan with unmatched energy and joy.
  • 🎶 Grand Finale – DJ, Band, Bharath bringing the community together.

🌸 Our Mission

  • Preserve traditions for the next generation.
  • Unite youth in culture and devotion.
  • Spread happiness, positivity, and service.
💐💐💐

🕰 Our Journey

1997 — Foundation

JDSS Youth begins in Kodimail to celebrate Maa Durga and Lord Ganesha with unity.

2005 — Growth

Community expands, more volunteers join, cultural programs flourish.

2015 — Initiatives

Structured organization, youth mentorship, and larger festival arrangements.

2025 — Today & Beyond

Digitally connected, transparent team structure, and inclusive celebrations.

📖 Our Story

In a small lane of Kodimail, a few friends gathered in 1997 and lit the first diya together. That light grew…
Each year brought new faces, louder bhajans, and stronger bonds. Elders guided, youth organized.
Today, JDSS Youth is a family — celebrating, serving, and smiling together.

🙏 Lord Ganesh Blessings 🙏

Om Gam Ganapataye Namah – The remover of obstacles and bestower of wisdom.

Sacred Ganesh Mantra

ॐ गं गणपतये नमः
Om Gam Ganapataye Namah

"Salutations to Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles"

🔎 Symbolism

Elephant head represents wisdom, large ears to listen to prayers, small mouth to speak less.

🎉 Celebration

10-day festival marking Ganesh's birth, ending with immersion (Visarjan) in water.

Celebrated with devotion since 1997, bringing community together in joy.
